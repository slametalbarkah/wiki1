Bhai Kot

Bhai Kot (Chak No. 03) is a village in the Pattoki Tehsil, Kasur District, Punjab, Pakistan, about 3 kilometres (1.9 mi) from Pattoki. Most of the population of this village consists of Meos. Other clans include Jats, Arains, and Dogars but are in a minority. There are two primary schools in the village, one for girls and one for boys.
