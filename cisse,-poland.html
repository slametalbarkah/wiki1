Cisse, Poland

Cisse [ˈt͡ɕissɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Szczutowo, within Sierpc County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 8 km (5 mi) north-west of Sierpc and 125 km (78 mi) north-west of Warsaw.
