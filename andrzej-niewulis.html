Andrzej Niewulis

Andrzej Niewulis (born 21 April 1989) is a Polish professional footballer who plays as a centre-back for and captains II liga club Zagłębie Sosnowiec.





== Career ==

In June 2010, he was loaned to Ruch Radzionków from Jagiellonia Białystok. He returned one year later. In July 2011, he was loaned to Wigry Suwałki.
