Aleksandr Kurnayev

Aleksandr Aleksandrovich Kurnayev (Russian: Александр Александрович Курнаев; born 1 July 1985) is a former Russian professional football player.





== Club career ==

He played in the Russian Football National League for FC Dynamo Saint Petersburg in 2010.
