Agh Barzeh

Agh Barzeh (Persian: اغ برزه, also Romanized as Āgh Barzeh; and Āghbarzeh) is a village in Shenetal Rural District, Kuhsar District, Salmas County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. According to the 2006 census, its population was 509, residing in 92 families.
