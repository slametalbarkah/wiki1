Antonio Sampietro

Antonio Sampietro Casarramona (born March 30, 1949, in Barcelona) was the third Mayor-President of Ceuta, one of Spain's Plazas de soberanía on the coast of North Africa. He held the post from August 26, 1999, until February 7, 2001. He was also the Mayor of Marbella from (1991-1995) and was recognised as a great leader to the Costa del Sol. As the Mayor of Marbella, he built San Jose School and the very well known “Paseo Maritimo” which connects Guadalmina to Puerto Banus. Antonio Sampietro was also involved in famous projects such as Starlite.
