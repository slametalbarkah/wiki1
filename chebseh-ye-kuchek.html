Chebseh-ye Kuchek

Chebseh-ye Kuchek (Persian: چبسه كوچك, also Romanized as Chebseh-ye Kūchek; also known as Chepseh-ye Kūchek and Chopseh-ye Kūchek) is a village in Elhayi Rural District, in the Central District of Ahvaz County, Khuzestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 306, in 58 families.
